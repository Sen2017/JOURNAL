V.A.C. Review

Our school's counseling office is not the most space friendly and when it comes to servicing large volumes of students, the counseling offices become flooded with students with barely enough room to shuffle by. This can cut a lot of students off from the support they need to help them get through their years at highschool. BetterComm is a mobile app that aims to improve the connection between counseling staff and students in a school environment. I'd improve our video by focussing more on features than purpose. It would greatly improve our video to have a rundown of all the features and a graphical concept of what those features would look like on the mobile platform. Bettercomm is currently slated to have the following features: appointment making, counselor chat rooms with the ability to remain annonymous, and push notifications that can remind students of their appointments. Counselors could also make their own announcement and have Bettercomm send them to the student(s) via a push notification. I'd like to add desktop compatibility for counselors to be able to work in an environment more familiar to them. A challenge my team and I faced was to find time to meet together. I wasn't available till after 3:30 and most of my teamates need to leave campus by 300. No offense to my great team members, but next time, I'd like to create content with people I know and have worked with in the past. I feel this would greatly increase productivity and since we travel in the same circles, we're likely to be available at the same times.
